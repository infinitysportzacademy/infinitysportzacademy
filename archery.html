    Archery is a sport or skill that involves using a bow to shoot arrows at a target. It has a long history and has been practiced for various purposes, including hunting and warfare, as well as for recreational and competitive purposes. Archery requires a combination of physical skill, mental focus, and precision. Archery is practiced in various forms, such as target archery, where participants shoot arrows at stationary targets set at different distances, and field archery, where archers navigate a course with targets set in various terrains. It is also an Olympic sport, featuring events such as individual and team competitions.
